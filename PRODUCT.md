# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal commerce operators who review, approve, reject, edit, and create community deals. They are working in a task-heavy environment and need to move quickly without losing confidence in what the system is doing.

## Product Purpose

Provide a focused operations console for managing community commerce deals through a typed mock API. The product exists to help operators inspect queue state, make review decisions, and keep deal workflows moving with clear feedback and reliable state boundaries.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, operator-first.

The interface should feel dependable, efficient, and matter-of-fact. It should reduce friction, not call attention to itself.

## Anti-references

- Marketing-style SaaS dashboards with ornamental hero sections and empty polish.
- Playful or whimsical commerce UI that undercuts operational seriousness.
- Over-decorated controls, novelty navigation, or custom affordances for standard tasks.
- Glassy, noisy, or gradient-heavy treatment that competes with the work.
- Inconsistent component patterns across list, detail, and form screens.

## Design Principles

- Make the next action obvious on every screen.
- Optimize for scanning, comparison, and decision-making.
- Keep interaction patterns consistent across the app.
- Show state clearly and immediately after every mutation.
- Favor familiar task-oriented UI over visual novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, strong keyboard support, semantic structure, and reduced-motion-friendly interactions. No special accommodation requirements are known beyond that baseline.
